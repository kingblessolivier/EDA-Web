About Us

EDO (Espoir de l’Avenir) is a Non-Governmental Organization, operating in Rwanda since 2014 and officially recognized by the Rwanda Governance Board (RGB) under the authorization No. 223.RGB/NGO/19/04/2018.

Our mission is to positively impact communities through charity and collaboration, guided by Islamic values of compassion and support. We provide training and support to individuals, families, and communities to improve their lives and contribute to the well-being of the community. We work with local and international partners to provide solutions that address local challenges and promote social and economic development.

We are a team of dedicated volunteers who are committed to providing high-quality training and support to individuals, families, and communities. We believe that education is the key to unlocking the potential of every individual and that together we can create a better future for our communities.

Explore Our Comprehensive Training Programs

EDO offers a variety of training programs designed to empower individuals with essential skills and knowledge. Our programs are tailored to meet the needs of diverse communities, focusing on personal development and vocational training.
