Discover EDO's Mission and Values

1. Introduction

EDO (Espoir de l’Avenir) is a Non-Governmental Organization, operating in Rwanda since 2014 and officially recognized by the Rwanda Governance Board (RGB).

2. Our Vision

To lead innovative initiatives that provide effective services and programs to the community.

3. Our Mission

Our mission focuses on education and welfare, specifically:

  • Empowering youth through vocational training.
  • Advocating for vulnerable populations both locally and internationally.

4. Key Initiatives

  • Vocational training programs to create employment opportunities for youth.
  • Support for projects aimed at improving the welfare of vulnerable populations.

5. Our Slogan

“Together for our better future.”
