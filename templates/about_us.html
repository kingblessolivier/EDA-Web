Discover EDA's Mission and Values

1. Introduction

ORGANIZATION «ESPOIR DE L'AVENIR» ("Hope For The Future") is a Non-Governmental Organization, operating in Rwanda since 2014 and officially recognized by the RWANDA GOVERNANCE BOARD (RGB) by the final authorization No. 223/RGB/NGO/LP/04/2018.

2. Our Vision

Leadership, innovation, and performance initiative to provide services and programs to the community through a strategic system efficient and effective.

3. Our Mission

The main Mission of our Organization is Education and Welfare based on:

  • Frame the youth in the small technical creation of employment and positive values
  • Study of projects for the vulnerable and advocacy inside and outside of the country for their welfare

4. Key Initiatives

  • Vocational Training to the Young people and Supervision for that, help them to create different employment and adapt themselves in new situations.
  • Study different projects for vulnerable people and look for them who can support these projects inside and outside the country for their social welfare.

5. Our Slogan

“Together for our better future.”
